Cranberry Salad

Grandma Gurgel's recipe, she got this from Mrs. Harkner of Milston along with buckets of cranberries right from the bogs. 3 cups ground cranberries (best to toss frozen in blender to chop) 1 cup crushed pineapple drained, 21 marshmallows cut in pieces or the equivalent amount of minis 3 apples ground, with peel (toss in with cranberries as you grind them) 1 cup sugar Mix all, just before serving fold in 1/2 cup whipped cream or cool whip.
