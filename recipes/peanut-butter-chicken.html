Peanut Butter Chicken

No real story behind this recipe. Found it on a jar of peanut butter Matt left when he went to India for the first time. Tried it and we liked it.  This is good if you add chopped peanuts for extra crunch. Stir fry chopped chicken breasts. Set aside. Combine and heat the following. Pour over chicken and hot rice. 1/3 cup peanut butter 1tbspn garlic pwdr 2T lemon juice 2T  soy sauce 1 tspn sugar 1/3 cup water sprinkle of cayenne pepper to  taste
