Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies cream: 3/4 cup margerine or butter 1 cup granulated sugar 1 cup packed brown sugar add: 1/2 cup peanut butter 2 eggs 2 tsp vanilla Mix well and then sift and add to cream mixture: 2 1/2 cups flour 1 tsp baking soda 1/2 tsp salt stir in 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips Bake at 350 degrees 10-12 minutes.
