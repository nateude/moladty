Easy Drop or Rolled Biscuits

I have not tried this recipe. Chris shared it with me. It call for oil, no cutting in of the shortening is needed. In bowl mix: 2 c. flour 1 T. bkg pwdr 1/3 c. oil 2/3 c. milk Stir until mixture forms ball. Knead 18-20 times. Roll, pat or drop by tablespoon onto sheet. Bake in 450 degree oven- no time given, I'm guessing 10 min. If you try it, let the rest of us know how you like it. Thanks
