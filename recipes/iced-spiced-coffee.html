Iced Spiced Coffee

4 quarts strong hot coffee add: 10 whole cloves 2 stick cinnamon Let steep for several hours add 1 pint cream Stir and serve over lots of ice This is an awsome cold coffee drink.  I added the spices to the pot as I made the coffee.  It didn't steep as long as the receipe said because Matthew said if I didn't make it soon he was going to drink hot.  I used one can of evaporated milk instead of the cream and then blended the coffee mix and ice together.    Squirt on a dollop of whipped cream or add a splash of chocolate syrup.  A great cool way to wake up your day!
