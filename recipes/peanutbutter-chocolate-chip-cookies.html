Peanutbutter Chocolate Chip Cookies

cream: 1/2 cup butter 1/2 cup peanut butter 1/2 cup packed brown sugar 1/4 cup sugar add: 1 egg 1 Tbsp milk 1 tsp vanilla sift: 1 cup flour 1/2 tsp bkg pwdr 1/2 tsp salt add to cream mixture:  stir in 1 cup chocolate chips 3/4 cup chopped peanuts Drop on cookie sheet: bake 9-11 min at 350   This is from the Ghirardelli Chocolate Chip bag.  Much better the other reciepe I was using.   This is being submitted at Laura's request. Happy baking
