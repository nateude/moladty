D'Amico & Sons Snickerdoodles

Cream: 1 cup unsalted butter 1 3/4 cup sugar Add: 2 large eggs Mix and add; 3 cups flour 1 1/2 tsp cream of tartar 3/4 tsp bkg soda 1/2 tsp salt Mix with cream mixture. Roll into golf size balls, roll in cinnamon sugar, place on greased cookie sheet and flatten slightly, half way down. Bake at 375 for 13-15 minutes, until lightly browned at edges and centers have puffed up: they will not look fully baked, cool on baking sheet.
