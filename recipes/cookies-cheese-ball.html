Cookies Cheese Ball

Mandy served this one Christmas. I am not sure why we need to add calories to cookies, but give this a try. You will like it. She served it with a tin of store bought butter cookies and I think, also, Teddy Grahams. Mix together: 8 oz softened cream cheese 1/2 c softened butter 1/4 t vanilla 3/4 c pwdr sugar 2 T brown sugar 3/4 c mini-chocolate chips. (I can see mini-M&M's in it also) Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours. Roll into ball and into chopped pecans if desired. Serve with graham crackers and shortbread cookies.
