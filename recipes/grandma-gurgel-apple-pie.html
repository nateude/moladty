Grandma Gurgel's Apple Pie

Mix: 1/4 cup sugar 3/4 tsp cinnamon 1/8 tsp salt 4 cup sliced , peeled and cored  apples  ( I usually make it closer to 6) Place in unbaked pie crust.  MIx: 3/4 cup brown sugar 3/4 cup flour 1/3 cup butter ( cut butter in with knife) sprinkle over apple mixture Bake at 400  for 30-40 min or until apples are fork tender.
