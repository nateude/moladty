Waffle Cookies

Mix all together: 1/2 cup melted butter 2 beaten eggs 3/4 cup sugar 6 Tblsp cocoa 1 cup flour 1 tsp vanilla Pour 1 Tblsp of batter into each section of the waffle iron. Bake 1 1/2 min Frost while warm Frosting 1/2 c chocolate chips melted 3 Tblsp cream or evaporated milk 1 cup powdered sugar mix all until smooth. This came from the Messiah, Hales Corners cookbook from Erica Braun. They taste like donuts and one batch is never enough! Add sprinkles before the frosting sets to make festive.
