Apple-Cinnamon Syrup

Boil until thick: 1/2 cup packed brown sugar 1/3 cup water 2Tblsp butter 1 tsp corn startch (add to sugar and stir before adding water) 1/2 tsp cinnamon When thick stir in 3 medium apples, peeled, cored and thinly sliced.  Continue cookint until apples are  tender. Serve over waffles or pancakes.
