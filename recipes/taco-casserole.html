Taco Casserole

Another easy and delicious recipe from the kitchen of Jess Ude. Brown 1 lb of ground beef add and brown: chopped onion green peppter garlic stir in 1 envelope taco seasoning In greased casserole dish spread 1 cup of crushed tortilla chips layer on 1 can (16 oz) of refried beans top with 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese Bake uncovered at 375 for 15-20 min. Garnash with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes sour cream, salsa, etc.
