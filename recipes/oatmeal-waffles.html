Oatmeal Waffles

Country magazine gets the credited for this filling waffle recipe. Grated apples are tasty in these and I have used blueberries as well. There is nothing tricky to making them. In mixing bowl combine: 1 1/2 c flour 1 c quick cooking oats (use the quick the regular don't cook thoroughly) 1/2 t cinnamon 1/4 t salt In a second bowl combine: 2 eggs, slightly beaten 1 1/2 c milk 6 T vegetable oil or melted butter 2 T brown sugar Stir the wet mixture into dry and bake in waffle iron. Top with brown sugar syrup or other syrup of choice.
