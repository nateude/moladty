Cornmeal Griddle Cakes

The history of this recipe is fairly short. I came across it somewhere and made them and liked them. Since Hales Corners I had lost track of the recipe. Gwen Koch mentioned that she loved the cornmeal griddle cake recipe she had gotten from me. I got it back. Still don't know where or which cookbook it came from.  Now I am posting it so I don't lose it again. Combine: 1 cup boiling water 3/4 cup cornmeal Add: 2 T molasses 1 cup buttermilk Beat in: 2 eggs Add: 2 T melted butter Mix in separate bowl 1 1/2 cups flour 1 T  bkg pwdr 1 1/2 t salt 1/4 t soda Mix into cornmeal and buttermilk Fry on greased griddle
