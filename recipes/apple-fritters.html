Apple Fritters

Ingredients

  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup finely chopped or grated
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons orange jucie
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder

Combine: egg, milk, chopped apple (I always put in more), sugar, orange jucie, tsp vanilla

stir together flour,baking powder

Mix into apple mixture

Drop by teaspoon into hot oil. Fry until deep golden brown turning once. Drain on brown paper and dip in sugar, cinnamon sugar or sift powder sugar over the tops
