Bird's Nest Cookies

Combine in large bowl and let sit: 3 cups crispy rice cereal 1 cup shredded coconut Mix and bring to just boiling: 1/3 cup light corn syrup 1/2 cup brown sugar 3/4 cup peanut butter 1 tsp vanilla Remove from heat and pour over cereal and coconut.  Shape into bird's nests.  I push the mixture into the mini-cup cake pans lined with papers.  You have to really pack well and let sit to coo.  Fill depression with jelly beans 'eggs'.
