Mom's Molasses Cookies

There are many ginger snap recipes out there and I have tried most of them. I checked out at cookbook that had recipes from Bed and Breakfast places in the midwest. This is from the Nash House B&B in Wisconsin Rapids. To keep the cookies chewy use shortening (Criso type), do not substitute butter or maragerine. Cream: 3/4 cup shortening 1 egg 1/4 cup molasses 1 cup sugar Mix and add: 2 cups flour 2 tsp bkg soda 1 tsp salt 1 tsp cinnamon 1 tsp cloves 1 tsp ginger Mix well, dough will be stiff.
